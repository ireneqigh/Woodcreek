Beautiful two bedroom apartments in Ypsilanti, MI.


Take a step in and be greeted by a sophisticated red carpet that leads to the place you call home. We offer recently upgraded apartments with two large bedrooms with walk-in closets, and one bathroom. Each apartment features an open living & dining room, and kitchen with a stove and refrigerator. The buildings are also equiped with washers and dryers.

Convenient Location

Close to everything you need—grab pizza, make an easy run to the bank, or pick up some household items—just a quick five minute drive away. Stroll around in the nearby parks, or enjoy the night out in Depot Town or Midtown. We are also close to Eastern Michigan University and Washtenaw Community College.
